Scandinavian Style Interiors

Scandinavian design in green

Botanical colour inspires this bunch of four Scandinavian style homes. Each contemporary interior space has been either coloured with lots of green decor or is home to revitalising living greenery. The array of indoor plants bring texture to the clean modern arrangements, and freshness to some neutral backdrops.

Scandinavian design in blue

Bright blue color and Scandinavian style is not a very common mix. Gray, white – yes, we can often meet them in the design of Swedish apartments, but blue is something new. And it should be noted, the experiment was a success – this apartment in shades of blue presents the Scandinavian design in a new light. We have to say that main color scheme was diluted with burgundy accents, which added a twist to this budget accommodation in one of the sleeping districts of Gothenburg.
